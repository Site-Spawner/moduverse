What is Moduverse?

Moduverse is a collaborative modular standard, ensuring stability and common ground when building LEGO layouts.

The standard originated from the Finnish LEGO User Group (LUG) Palikkatakomo but has since spread throughout Scandinavia.

Today it’s the official layout standard in Plikkatakomo and Swebrick (Swedish LUG) and gaining ground in Brikkelauget (Norwegian LUG) and Byggepladen (Danish LUG).
